<objective>
Complete reference for all {{variable}} types used in cold email personalization. Variables are placeholders that get replaced with prospect-specific data.
</objective>

<formatting_rules>
**Always use `{{double_braces}}`**
- {{first_name}} ✅
- {first_name} ❌ (single braces)
- {{firstName}} ❌ (use snake_case)

**Never invent facts**
- If you don't have the data, omit the variable
- Don't guess or hallucinate values

**Match your data source**
- Variable names should match your Clay/data column names
- Consistency prevents merge errors
</formatting_rules>

<ai_generated_variables>
**Dynamic variables generated by AI based on context:**

| Variable | Description | Generated From |
|----------|-------------|----------------|
| {{ai_customer_description}} | Description of who they sell to | Website, LinkedIn description |
| {{ai_customer_type}} | Type of customer they target | Website analysis |
| {{ai_generation}} | Flexible contextual generation | Various sources |

**Examples:**
- {{ai_customer_description}}: "fitness enthusiasts who want to breathe better"
- {{ai_customer_type}}: "VPs of Finance" or "professional men looking for classic styles"
- {{ai_generation}}: Topic extracted from recent post, insight from pricing page

**How to generate:**
Use Claygent or similar AI to analyze their website/LinkedIn and extract:
- Who their customers are
- What problems they solve
- What industry/vertical they serve
</ai_generated_variables>
