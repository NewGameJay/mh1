<template_type>
Custom Signal Campaign - Email 1
</template_type>

<when_to_use>
Use when you have strong research data: case studies, pricing insights, G2 reviews, hiring signals, or other specific observations about the prospect.
</when_to_use>

<structure>
```
Subject: [2-4 words, intriguing]

{{First_name}}—[Situation recognition with {{custom_signal}}]

[Value prop + proof in 1-2 sentences with {{case_study_metric}}]

[Optional: "Specifically, it looks like you're trying to sell to {{ai_customer_type}}..."]

[Low-effort CTA]
```
</structure>

<template_a>
**Standard Custom Signal**

```
Subject: [2-4 words]

{{First_name}}—[observation with {{custom_signal}}].

[Question that pokes the bear about their current situation]

[Metric from case study: "We helped {{case_study_customer_type}} achieve {{case_study_metric}}."]

Worth a look?
```

**Example filled:**
```
Subject: Quick question

{{First_name}}—saw you hired 4 SEs last quarter.

Are you building custom workflows for every enterprise deal, or is there a plan to productize those patterns?

We helped similar teams 3x deal volume without adding headcount.

Worth a look?
```
</template_a>

<template_b>

[Case study proof: "We've attributed {{case_study_metric}} for {{case_study_customer_type}}."]
