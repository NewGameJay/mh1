## About This Course

About this course

Growth isn't about individual parts but the alignment between your Market, Product, Model, Brand, and Channel. When these "fits" work together, the entire system compounds and creates sustainable growth that feels effortless.

The framework provides practical filters for decision-making. Product-Market Fit establishes core value alignment, Model-Market Fit ensures financial viability, and Product-Channel Fit matches your product to channel rules. Brand-Market Fit amplifies everything else, while layered filtering helps you systematically eliminate unsuitable channels and focus on what actually works for your specific business configuration.
