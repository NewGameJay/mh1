## Page 1: Executive Summary & Key Findings

### Executive Summary

Swimply is the leading peer-to-peer marketplace for hourly rentals of pools, courts, and outdoor amenities. Founded in 2018 by Bunim Laskin, the company has raised $63.2M in venture funding and facilitated over 4 million experiences across 15,000+ hosts in 150+ cities. Often called the "Airbnb for pools," Swimply has evolved from pure pool rentals to include hot tubs, pickleball courts, tennis courts, basketball courts, and full home rentals by the hour.

### Key Findings

1. **Market Leader Position:** First-mover advantage in pool sharing economy with 15,000+ hosts across US, Canada, and Australia
2. **Strong Unit Economics:** Commission model (10% guest fee + 15% host fee) with $25M annual revenue (2023)
3. **Pandemic Accelerator:** 4,000% growth during COVID-19 as pools became safe social gathering spaces
4. **Product Expansion:** Successfully diversified beyond pools into courts, hot tubs, igloos, and full homes
5. **Seasonal Business Model:** ~70% of bookings occur May-August, requiring off-peak optimization strategies
